Engage and inform your customers with interactive kiosks

We can help you design a better way to get information directly into the hands of your customers. Interactive kiosks can entertain, educate, engage customers, and allow for exploration of large amounts of information. The systems we build follow a thoroughly thought out process that allows for easy updating of content and can be used as a foundation for more content to be added over the years.

There are a number of unique opportunities to make information more accessible — literally at the fingertips of your clients, customers, visitors, and guests — using interactive kiosk technology. When it comes to custom kiosk development, the sky’s the limit in what we can dream up together. Innovative and effective uses for interactive kiosks include:

  • Outdoor or indoor usage
  • Mobile or free-standing
  • Scalable in size, from life size to handheld
  • Robust and sturdy for use in areas of heavy foot traffic
  • Perfect for lobbies, waiting rooms, transitional spaces, recreation areas, places of learning, landmarks and historical sites, museums, libraries, transportation centers, restaurants, health care facilities and more

Consider how an interactive kiosk could become a force multiplier in your customer engagement or communications strategy. Truly, if you can dream it, we can build it.
